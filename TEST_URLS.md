## ‚úÖ What Makes a Good Wikipedia URL?

- **Standard English Wikipedia:** `https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/...`
- **Well-established articles:** Popular topics work best
- **Good article structure:** Articles with clear sections
- **Substantial content:** Articles with enough text (not stubs)
